Our Story

Bean Bliss Café was founded in 2023 by Khawaja Rohan Waheed, a passionate coffee lover who wanted to bring the best coffee to town. With a journey that started in the highlands of Ethiopia, Khawaja's commitment to quality and ethically sourced beans has made Bean Bliss a local favorite.
